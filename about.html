Hi! I am Sasha Singh. I am currently a Computer Science major and an Entrepreneurship and Psychology minor at the University of Utah. I spent the first 12 years of my life in New Delhi, India and the rest have been spent traveling to major cities all over the United States, which is one of the reasons I am extroverted and open to communicating with people from all walks of life and backgrounds. Currently I am located in Salt Lake City, the home of the greatest snow on earth, and I am lucky to call it home. I am also very lucky to be in the midst of Silicon Slopes, Startup culture, and the technology revolution as we see how the world around is changing. I come from a family of engineers, it was only fitting for me to fall in my parents footsteps and work as a Software Engineer on projects I find fascinating, whether that be in the Instagram division of Meta to analyze their explore pages, or at a FinTech that diversifies credit card reward programs based on consumer spending.
